A Legacy of Excellence and Discipline

The Story of Our School

Namiryango High School is a prestigious and trusted institution in Uganda, known for its strong foundation in discipline, academic excellence, and moral values.

Established in 2004 under the leadership of Dr Busulwa Henry Ssebuliba with the purpose of educating citizens in arts and science subjects and also to nuture morally compassed individuals.

Our Foundation

Built on strong catholic values, the school emphasizes integrity, hard work, and respect. These principles continue to guide students in both academic and personal growth.

Growth and Achievement

Over the years, Namiryango High School has grown in both size and reputation. The institution has embraced modern education while preserving its core values hence gaining the title of the school of great achievers.

Our Vision

To continue shaping future leaders through discipline, innovation, and a commitment to excellence.
